Privacy

Hangout · last updated July 2026 · contact shiv@yourmedicals.co.uk

We never store where you live. Not encrypted, not private — we don't ask for it and there is no field for it. You give us a postcode district ("LE18"); we store the centre of that district moved a random few hundred metres. That's enough to answer "is this game within 10 miles of me" and useless for finding anyone's front door.

What we hold

  • Your first name and initials.
  • Your phone number, to sign you in.
  • A fuzzed point near your postcode district. Never your address, never GPS.
  • The sports you play and, for racket sports, your level.
  • Games you've joined, hosted or been on the waitlist for.
  • Messages you send in a game's group chat.
  • Whether you turned up — the count that appears as "turned up to 12 of 13".

What other people can see

Before someone is in a game, they cannot see the venue, the court, who is playing, or the group chat. They see the sport, the time, how far away it is, roughly which district, the cost and how many spots are left. That's enforced by the database, not by the app.

In "people near you", others see your first name, your sports and a distance band — "under a mile" — never an exact number. Three exact distances triangulate to an address; three bands don't. You can turn yourself off entirely with Let people find me.

What we never do

  • Sell your data. Ever.
  • Share it with advertisers.
  • Track you across other apps or websites.
  • Store your real location, as above.

We may publish aggregate counts — "47 people in LE2 want padel" — to help decide which sports to open where. That's a number, never a person.

Deleting everything

In the app: You → Delete my account. It's immediate, it removes your profile, your messages and any games you host, and it cannot be undone. You don't have to email anyone.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

You can ask for a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it — email shiv@yourmedicals.co.uk and we'll answer within 30 days. Our lawful basis is legitimate interest in running the service, and consent for notifications. Data is held in the EU/UK. If you're unhappy you can complain to the ICO.

Reporting people

Every profile has Block and Report. We read reports within 24 hours and remove people who abuse the service. Reporting someone blocks them immediately — you shouldn't have to keep seeing them while we look.

18+

Hangout is for adults. We don't knowingly hold data on under-18s; if you believe we do, email us and we'll delete it.
